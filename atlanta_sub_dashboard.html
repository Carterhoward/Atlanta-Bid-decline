Finding 1 — subscription reality

Of the 197 subs whose bid activity went to zero for two straight months, only 21% have actually cancelled. 67% are still paying, active subscriptions — they didn't leave, they went quiet. That's a renewal conversation waiting to happen, not a lost account.
Finding 2 — how much warning do you actually get

We measured the gap between "last time a sub looked healthy" (bidding at ≥50% of their own peak pace) and the month they went fully dark. It's short — and it's shorter for your bigger accounts.
~2 monthsHigh/mid-volume subs: median time from crossing the 50%-of-peak line to zero bids (about 60 days)
~3 monthsLow-volume/sporadic subs: median lag (about 90 days) — noisier, less predictable
68%of all churns happen within one quarter of that first crossing
